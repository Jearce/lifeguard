Returning guards

Coming back to work another great summer? Awesome, glad to have you back. Remember, you will either need a Refresher course to refresh your skills before you get back on the stand, or your certification is about to expire and you will need a Review Class to renew your certification for another 2 years.

Refresher class

The Refresher Class is a 1 hour class meant to refresh lifeguarding, first aid, and CPR skills. You must hold a current American Red Cross Lifeguard Certification in order to take the class. The Refresher is meant for returning lifeguards.

Review class

The Review class is a 2 day class for participants who are currently certified and about to expire. You have 30 days from expiration to qualify for a Review Class. After that, you must take the entire Lifeguard Certification Class.
